ExpenseX

ExpenseX is a sleek and intuitive expense tracker designed to help you take full control of your finances. With ExpenseX, you can effortlessly log your daily expenses, categorize them, and monitor your spending trends over time.

The app offers insightful visual reports, including monthly bar graphs, category-wise breakdowns with percentages, and a clear overview of your total expenses. Whether you want to track personal spending, manage budgets, or stay on top of borrowings, ExpenseX provides a simple, user-friendly interface that makes financial management easy and stress-free.
